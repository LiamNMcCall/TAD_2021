
Formula for text-as-data research 


What question do you want to answer? This should be something you *actually* want to know, and it should end in a question mark.
Does media coverage of Indigenous American reservation life skew negative or positive?

What source of text data will you use?
News articles with special weight added to headlines
What are two possible answers to the question?
Yes, media outlets tend to place excess emphasis on alcoholism and sexual/domestic violence or No, media outlets have focused on hope and economic development 
How will the results of the quantitative text analysis demonstrate which of those answers is correct?
Through training the machine to determine which words ("ADDICTION, "VIOLENCE") or ("HOPE", "OPPORTUNITY") feature more in both headlines and articles.
